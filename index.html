Who we are & What we do

The Ddabble community is a non-profit organisation which promotes mass adoption and innovation of Blockchain technology among youth, through equipping individuals with emerging technology skill sets, which would accelerate innovation within one’s learning path, and make them future-ready.


Our core philosophy is to use the power of self-learning and peer mentoring within communities inside college campuses and institutions to achieve this outcome. We are currently based out of Kerala, India.
